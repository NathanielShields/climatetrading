What the strategy actually does

1 · Detect

Daily, fuse soil-moisture z-scores (Open-Meteo + SMAP), 10-day rain probability, pollination-window heat stress, NDVI greenness anomaly, and USDA Good/Excellent drops into a single drought-confirmation flag.

2 · Enter

On confirmation, buy an ATM straddle on the active CME corn contract (auto-rolled per the H/K/N/U/Z calendar). Margin account caps premium at 25% of capital.

3 · Manage & attribute

Mark daily with Black-76. Take a partial profit when IV spikes; close on confirmed weather recovery or expiry. Decompose every day's P&L into futures, vol, theta, and cross-gamma via full re-pricing.
